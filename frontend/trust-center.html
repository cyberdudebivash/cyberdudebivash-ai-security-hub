AI / LLM Inference

Conversational AI features (AI Copilot, AI verdicts, executive summaries) send prompt content — which may include chat messages, scan targets, and scan findings — to one inference provider per request, selected by our provider router with automatic fallback: Groq (US), Cloudflare Workers AI (US/edge), and — only where configured — DeepSeek (China), OpenRouter (US), Together AI (US), Anthropic (US). Deterministic features (scan scoring, template-based rule generation, CVE ingestion) do not call LLM providers.
