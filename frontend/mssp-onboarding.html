Frequently Asked Questions
What does white-label mean for my clients? ▾
Silver and Gold partners get a custom domain, your logo on all dashboards and reports, and zero CYBERDUDEBIVASH® branding visible to your clients. It looks like your own product.
Can I upgrade from Reseller to Silver mid-month? ▾
Yes. Upgrades are prorated and take effect immediately. Your client accounts and data carry over with no downtime.
What does the 40%/50% margin mean? ▾
You set your own client pricing. The margin percentage represents the revenue share structure on sub-licensing and co-branded resale arrangements. Direct clients billed by you have no margin restrictions.
Is there a minimum client commitment? ▾
No minimum client requirement. The Reseller plan supports up to 10 clients, Silver up to 50, Gold unlimited. You only need to maintain your monthly subscription.
What payment methods are accepted? ▾
All payments are processed via Razorpay. UPI, NetBanking, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), and EMI options are available.
Is my trial data preserved if I upgrade? ▾
Yes. All client accounts, reports, and configurations created during your 14-day trial are preserved when you upgrade to a paid plan.
