FAQ
Common Questions
Do I need to give you access to my production systems? ▾
No production access is required for most engagements. We work from documentation, architecture diagrams, API specs, and sandbox/staging environments. For Red Team engagements, we may request read-only sandbox access with your explicit written consent.
Is there a GST invoice provided? ▾
Yes. Every engagement includes a GST-compliant invoice from CYBERDUDEBIVASH Pvt. Ltd. with full GSTIN details. This can be used for input tax credit by your organization.
What AI systems do you cover? ▾
We cover all major LLM platforms (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, Mistral), agent frameworks (LangChain, CrewAI, AutoGen, OpenAI Agents SDK), RAG pipelines, custom fine-tuned models, AI APIs, chatbots, and MCP-based deployments.
How is payment handled? ▾
50% advance on booking confirmation, 50% on report delivery. We accept bank transfer (NEFT/RTGS/IMPS), UPI, and Razorpay (credit/debit card, net banking). Payment details are provided after the scoping call.
What if I need a custom scope? ▾
Book a free 30-minute scoping call and we'll design a custom engagement for your exact requirements. Custom scopes are priced based on complexity and asset count.
Is the report confidential? ▾
Yes, absolutely. We sign a mutual NDA before any engagement begins. All findings, data, and reports are strictly confidential and never shared with third parties.
Do you offer startup or NGO discounts? ▾
Yes. Early-stage startups (Series A and below) and registered NGOs are eligible for 20–30% discounts. Mention your organization type during the scoping call.
